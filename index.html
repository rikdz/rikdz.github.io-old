I am a Post-Doctoral researcher working for Professor Noah Smith and the Department of Computer Science at the University of Washington. I received my PhD from the UW Department of Linguistics where I was co-advised by Hannaneh Hajishirzi and Gina-Anne Levow.

Research Summary

I research machine learning techniques for document understanding and generation. In understanding, I focus on intertextuality for techincal documents, language grounding, and question answering. In generation, I focus on learning to write coherent multi-sentence texts which meet specified content and style goals.
